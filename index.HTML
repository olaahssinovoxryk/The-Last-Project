EXO

EXO is the name of a popular South Korean-Chinese boy band. The band debuted on April 8, 2012 under SM Entertainment and consisted the of 12 members.
The band does currently consists of 9 members: Suho, Xiumin, Lay, Baekhyun, Chen, Chanyeol, D.O., Kai and Sehun. The former members are Kris, Tao and Luhan.
The official name of the fandom is EXO-L and their color is Cosmic Latte.
